We are Team TADA: Trygg Ai og DataAnalyse

We enable Responsible AI and data science at the Norwegian Labour and Welfare Administration (Nav).

We provide feedback, guidance, and technical assistance, in addition to shaping policy and governance frameworks. We keep abreast of trends in AI/ML and data science by partnering with research institutions, participating in conferences and meetups, and exploring new technology and methods, sharing what we learn both inside and outside of Nav.

Research and projects we've been involved in:

  • ENACT
  • AI4Users
  • Common framework for ethical standards of AI in Public Employment Services

Events we've presented at or helped to arrange:

  • AI Fairness Cluster Conference & AIMMES Workshop

  • Mangfold i mai
